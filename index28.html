What is NASA?

Nasa stands for National Aeronautics and Space Administration. Nasa was established in October 1,1958. It is an independent agency that is held by the United States Federal Government and they are responsible for the space program. They study space exploration, space technology, space science, the Earth, and aeronautics research. This research is very helpful because we can study this and learn about this indformation. They also try to make discoveries about the space exploration. Nasa is responsible and in charge of U.S science and technology which is applied to the airplanes or space.
