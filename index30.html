What has NASA invented and launched?

NASA has made many things! So far, they have launched 166 crewed space mission rockets up in space. They have also launched thirteen X-15 rocket flights. NASA had their first launch in July 1950 from Cape Canaveral, Fla. There are many satellites that NASA has launched, for example the Hubble Space Telescope. NASA has invented things like firefighting equipment, space blankets, cochlear implant, and etc. All of these inventions and launches were successful and has helped us.
